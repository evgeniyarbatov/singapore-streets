# Linguistic Origin

- **English/British**: Aljunied Avenue 1-5, Aljunied Crescent, Aljunied Road, Aljunied Walk
- **Malay**: Alkaff Crescent, Amaranda Gardens
- **Chinese**: Allotment Gardens (可能来自于英文的"Allotment Garden")

# Historical Themes

- **Colonial Figures**: Allenby Road (named after Sir Edmund Allenby, British General)

# Nature & Geography

- **Plants/Trees**: Almond Avenue, Almond Crescent, Almond Street
- **Animals**: Alps Avenue (可能来自于英文的"Alps", 与动物无关)

# Descriptive

- **Colors**: Amber Close, Amber Gardens, Amber Road

# Numerical or Lettered Names

- **Numbered Streets/Avenues**: Aljunied Avenue 1-5
- **Lettered Names**: Allanbrooke Road (可能来自于英文的"Allan", 是一个名字而不是字母)

# Residential/Community Themes

- **Neighbourhood Concepts**: Ama Keng Road (可能来自于当地居民的名称)

# Modern Development

- **HDB-era Names**: AMK Central Heights, AMK Tech Link
